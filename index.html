  • What is Sunday School Online

    Sunday School Online is a place where Christian teachers can teach the core doctrines and creeds of the faith according to the Apostle’s Creed and the basic doctrines upon which 99% of the Christian faith agree. We believe these doctrines are the key to restoring the principles and practices of reasoning from the scripture in order to avoid the tendency of human civilization to walk the “Great Circle of History” where we make choices which look and result in remarkably similar ways from earlier in history. We seek to provide the knowledge of God; giving coming generations the tools to deal with end times scenarios impacting our society. We seek to bring them hope, faith and endurance to rise to leadership and strength in tumultuous times. We would like to present the doctrines in a new and modern way which answers the challenge of this time. We believe these videos are a great opportunity for Christian teacher, organizations and institutions to promote themselves.

  • How Does It Work

    Presentation Format

    This is a global platform, please keep your expressions clear and easy to understand even for those for whom English is not their first language. Assume your viewers are young Christians, or new to the faith and communicate with them without great references to scripture. You are introducing them to all these references. Use plain language, and universal references as if they had not been raised in the church and have no background from which to draw Christian reference.

    • Explain the Doctrine - Biblical Sources and Reference in detail
    • Explore its Implications - Why it is relevant today - How to apply it to modern thought
    • Explain why you believe and how it affects your life - How it impacted your life - How it has impacted the lives of others

    Presentation Styles

    Your video production can be presented through several methods which enhance teaching:

    • Lecture with Graphics and/or video
    • Talk show style with interviews
    • Animation with explanatory narrative
    • Drama with explanatory narrative

    Presentation Constraints

    • No more than 3-5 Minutes in Length - Lecture with Graphics and/or video
    • No more than 7 minutes in Length - Talk show style with interviews
    • No more than 3-7 minutes in Length - Animation with explanatory narrative
    • No more than 10 minutes in Length - Drama with explanatory narrative

    Promotional Allowances

    You may promote yourself, a product, service, or event at the beginning of the presentation with a show open or introduction lasting no more than 30 seconds and at the end of your presentation lasting no more than 30 seconds. You are allotted 1 minute of promotional time 30 seconds before and after the presentation, but your presentation must still stay within presentation duration constraints.

    Video Technical Specifications

    Your video presentation must be finished production content, produced with clear and edited audio and video without technical difficulties. Your presentation is part of the value of the platform, there can be nothing in the videos which distracts from the message. Here are our specifications:

    • Aspect Ratio: 1280 x 720
    • High Definition Video
    • .MOV or Uncompressed or Compressed for delivery as H.264
    • Use wetransfer.com to deliver the file to us

    Values Summary

    The keys to this platform are in our ability to refrain from division and criticism of other Christians. We should share the doctrines in their simplicity. Be honest about what doctrines have been impactful on our lives. Share your testimony of that impact. Do not teach what you have not lived or do not fully understand. Only teach what you can explain as to why you believe it and share a testimony of its direct impact on your life, the lives of others or the life of the church in general. Let every doctrine taught, be accompanied with a good testimony of its impact. It is in the implications and the revelations of His Word that we live out this life in Christ. These implications and revelations shared; will provide us with an endless source of teachings to share with the Body of Christ. In this manner, we can make this platform into a witness for Christ in the world and help one another to understand and experience the impact of reasoning and thinking from a Biblical foundation.
